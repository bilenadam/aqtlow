these folders are:

dTxfrTCP and dTxfrUSB
 Arduino libraries
  Put in /home/user/sketchbook/libraries

DigTemp
 Arduino program for Digital Temperature
  Put in /home/user/sketchbook
It depends on the dallastemperature and onewire libraries.
http://milesburton.com/Dallas_Temperature_Control_Library
http://www.pjrc.com/teensy/td_libs_OneWire.html

aQtLow
 QT app
  Put in QT projects folder

aQtLowData
 data files for QT app
  Put in /home/user 
  Or in windows C:\documents and settings\username
If the QT app can't find it, it will build a skeleton of it in the default user folder.
